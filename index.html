Notes, Assignments & Senior Gyaan — All in One Place

Hey Junior, Welcome to GuruCool 2.0

Starting an MBA can feel overwhelming — new subjects, new professors, new deadlines, new committees, new expectations, and a lot happening at once. GuruCool is here to make that journey a little easier.

What is GuruCool?

GuruCool is a student-led academic resource platform created to bring notes, assignments, PPTs, senior-curated resources, and useful academic guidance into one simple place. Instead of digging through endless WhatsApp messages and scattered Google Drive folders, juniors can find everything trimester-wise and subject-wise.

Why GuruCool Exists

We know how confusing the first few months can feel. GuruCool exists to hold your hand through that phase — not as a formal system, but as a friendly senior support space where resources, guidance, and experiences are shared openly.

Our Mission

To make academic navigation simpler for every junior by collecting important notes, assignments, PPTs, project references, and senior insights in one easy-to-access platform.

Our Vision

To build a culture where every batch supports the next batch, and academic resources flow smoothly from seniors to juniors year after year.

Our Values

Care Community Accessibility Simplicity Trust Collaboration No Junior Left Confused

Future Goal

In the future, GuruCool will allow students to upload their own notes, share project learnings, contribute resources, and pass on useful academic guidance to upcoming batches.

This is not just a resource website. It is a small academic safety net created by seniors for juniors.
